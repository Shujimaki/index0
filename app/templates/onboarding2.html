Earthquake Emergency Kit

About Yanig

Our Purpose

We are dedicated to providing a reliable and immediate emergency notification system focused on user safety during earthquakes.

Vision Statement

To establish the standard in rapid, reliable, and personalized earthquake notification, building a network of safety where every individual is instantly informed when an earthquake occurs, ultimately contributing to a culture of preparedness and resilience.

Statement From the Creators

We built this application because we understand the feeling of powerlessness when the earth shakes. Our commitment is simple: to transform uncertainty into preparation. By combining reliable official data with immediate notification to your emails, we aim to provide you and your loved ones with the crucial seconds needed to stay safe. Your security is our driving force.
